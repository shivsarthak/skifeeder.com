Established in the year 2007, we are a reputed manufacturer, exporter and supplier of Gripper, NC Servo Feeder, Pneumatic Feeder, Straightener ,Decoilers etc. Our range of products can also be customized as per the technical specifications provided to us by clients. Owing to their qualitative features, our assortment is widely appreciated by our esteemed customers based in Middle East, South East Asia, USA and many other countries.

Our dedicated team of personnel who works round the clock to achieve the organization’s goal of attaining utmost client satisfaction. Our team of engineers and technicians have an in-depth knowledge and years of experience in the industry, which helps us to fulfill the exact requirements of clients. The team upgrades the range as per their needs and also make sure that each product conforms with the highest standards of quality. Further, we also guarantee our clients with timely delivery assurance, as per their requirements.

Evolved into a performance leader within its respective industry over a period as a result of innovation and problem solving competence covering all aspects of Pneumatics, Mechanical and Electrical Automation Technology.

Despite operating as a small-scale unit and under a phased manufacturing programmed, SKI quickly gained a good market share and high growth in sales turnover, by stablishing a wide customer base. This was made possible on account of the versatile range of quality products and the technical support provided to users on free-of-charge basis. These services have been enhanced considerably over the years. The product range has been continuously expanded and updated to not only meet market requirements but also introduce state-of-the-art products into the country.

The major asset of any company is its people. Only through continuous training can people keep up with emerging technology and thereby give optimum return on the company’s investment. Our company has passed the international quality system management the ISO 9001:2008 authentication. Our service principle is to survive by quality and win customers with credit. The whole staffs will offer all-round service to gain your support with good quality, technology and service.
